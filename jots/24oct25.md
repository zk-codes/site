<p>Found this in the depths of my notes. Written several years ago.</p>
<blockquote>
    <p>dogs teeth encircling his joints</p>
    <p>more piercings than facial skin</p>
    <p>he shares his name with your favorite weather pattern</p>
    <p>like a meteorologist in love you think of him every time the word hits your ears</p>
    <p>is this how it is?</p>
    <p>going around in circles</p>
    <p>the kind of orbit that pulls tides</p>
    <p>and leaves salt in the cracks of your palms</p>
    <p>you trace the patterns he leaves behind</p>
    <p>like constellations you'll never name but always recognize</p>
    <p>his laughter is a storm breaking over the horizon</p>
    <p>the kind that washes everything clean</p>
    <p>he wears his history like a second skin</p>
    <p>scars and ink and metal all tangled together</p>
    <p>the sky outside is heavy with clouds</p>
    <p>you wonder if he's thinking of you too</p>
    <p>or if you're just another echo</p>
    <p>another ripple in a pond you didn't mean to disturb</p>
    <p>is this how it is?</p>
    <p>spinning in place while the world moves on</p>
    <p>holding onto something that's already gone</p>
</blockquote>
