<p>Here's a poem I found when clearing out notes. It's about Xander, from <a href="/books">Don't Burn Too Bright.</a></p>
<blockquote>
    <p>he wears his scars like he has something to prove</p>
    <p>has to declare his battleworthiness to the world</p>
    <p>so they don't threaten to shatter him again</p>
    <p>he's been torn apart by his nobilty</p>
    <p>stitched back together with the ashes of his demise</p>
    <p>he's watched the stars fall from their perches, the sun set itself alight and swallow the world he once treasured</p>
    <p>created to be a destroyer of worlds</p>
    <p>lost loves tattooed across his shoulders</p>
    <p>bandages against the universe's battering ram</p>
    <p>eyes like the burning volcano where he met his almost end</p>
    <p>and still</p>
    <p>he smiles</p>
</blockquote>
