My Approach

I believe in workshops that actually work. As a queer generalist, I create engaging sessions blending practical instruction with creative exploration.

Every workshop I run is:

  • Adaptable to various age groups and experience levels
  • Available online or in-person
  • Built around active participation rather than passive learning
  • Focused on adaptive exercises participants can immediately apply

I have a warm, generous, and enthusiastic presence. I'm fluent in English and intermediate in Spanish. One writing workshop attendees spoke Brazilian Portuguese and Spanish. The workshop was in English but I translated so she could participate.

Offerings

Creative Writing

Worldbuilding

  • Developing fictional settings through guided exercises
  • Exploring techniques for creating believable worlds

Zine-Making

  • Learning multiple zine formats and binding techniques
  • Creating a complete mini-zine during the workshop
  • Resources for continuing their zine practice
