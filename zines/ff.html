Fabled Fantasy

Published: 31 Mar 2025 | Updated: 31 Mar 2025

Selected fantasy book recommendations.

Want a copy? You can get one (a readable PDF, a printable PDF, and a text file) for $2+. Payable via bank transfer or cash (contact me), credit/debit card, donation, PayPal, or a trade.

You can also get one in print from Zine Gang Distro, or one in-person at Sticky Institute in Melbourne and G:Zines in Geelong, Australia.

  • Anything by Rick Riordan or Kay O'Neill is excellent!
  • Newt's Emerald by Garth Nix
  • In Deeper Waters by F.T. Lukens
  • The Wisteria Society Of Lady Scoundrels by India Holton
  • At Midnight by Dahlia Adler
  • Fake Blood by Whitney Gardner
  • One Year At Ellsmere by Faith Erin Hicks
  • The Princess And The Grilled Cheese Sandwich by Deya Muniz
  • The Wolf's Howl by A.L. Tait
  • Transmorgify by g. neri davis
  • The Sunbearer Trials by Aiden Thomas
  • Cemetery Boys by Aiden Thomas
  • Legends And Lattes by Travis Baldry
  • Garlic And The Witch by Bree Paulsen
  • So This Is Ever After by F.T. Lukens
  • Tidesong by Wendy Xu
  • Out Of The Blue by Jason June
  • Twelfth Grade Night by Molly Booth
  • Tim Te Maro And The Subterranean Heartsick Blues by H.S. Valley
  • Hungry Hearts by Elsie Chapman
  • Clash Of Steel by C.B. Lee
