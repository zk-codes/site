Zines

Published: 16 Sep 2024 | Updated: 10 Apr 2025

Herein you'll find my zine portfolio: all I've made so far, their title, a brief description, visuals, and where you can get a copy. They're handmade with reclaimed materials, or hand-drawn on my computer and digitally typeset. Enjoy!

They've been selected for inclusion in the State Library Victoria and National Library Of Australia catalog.

They're available to buy at Sticky Institute in Melbourne, Australia, G:Zines in Geelong, Australia, and online through Zine Gang Distro.

You can also get a copy (a readable PDF, a printable PDF, and a text file) for $2+. Payable via bank transfer or cash (contact me), credit/debit card, donation, PayPal, or a trade.
