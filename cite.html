How To Cite This Site

Written By: Zachary Kai » Published: 1 Feb 2026 | Updated: 1 Feb 2026

How to cite pages on my website, and how I cite personal sites on my blog.

How To Cite Pages On My Site

When citing a post on my site, refer to me and the site (as one entity) as Zachary Kai, Zach, or Zachary. My pronouns are he/him. If you have questions, contact me!

Academic-Style Referencing

Please use Zachary Kai as the author name and zacharykai.net as the site name.

How I Cite Personal Sites

I refer to a site's author by the name specified on their about page (or similar.) If you see your site mentioned and would prefer a different reference, contact me and I'll fix it!
