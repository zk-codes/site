Trades

I love trading things with folks (digitally or by mail) so...what are you waiting for? Contact me!

  • I can offer: ATCs, book recommendations, collages, doodles, floral/plant themed ephemera, handmade envelopes, letters, my zines, playlists, and written postcards.
  • Always happy to receive: ATCs, blank or written postcards, comics, drawings, ephemera, stickers, maps, pamphlets, pins, poetry, pressed leaves/flowers, washi tape, and zines.
  • Not looking for: CDs, DVDs, fabric, plants, patches, print books or photos, seeds, thread, vinyl records, or yarn.

As inspired by Kami and Ava, I'd also love to trade blog post titles!
