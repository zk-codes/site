Hello!

Published: 27 Jun 2024 | Updated: 1 Jun 2025

Here you'll find my contribution to /hello, compiled by Alastair Johnston. See more folks' here.

I'm seeking remote work! Here's my resume and CV. If I'd be a good fit for your organization or project, email me: hi@zacharykai.net or message me on Linkedin.

I run group workshops on drawing for fun, creative writing, worldbuilding and zine-making. Click the aforementioned link for more info and to book one for your group!

And if you have questions, want to trade with me, or say hello, please do so! I adore emails. hi@zacharykai.net. You can also send me a webmention, or check my FAQ. The chat networks I use are Discord (zachary.kai) and Signal. So if you prefer, message me!

Want to meet for a beverage? If I'm in your vicinity, I'd love to meet you! I'm Australian, but I'm not always there (sometimes I live overseas.) Please email or message me!

Everything I make worth sharing goes on the site. Here's the RSS Feed.

I'm on Github, the Indieweb, Internet Archive, Ko-Fi, and Linkedin. I'm not on social media.
